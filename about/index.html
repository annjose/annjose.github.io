About Me

I’m Ann Catherine Jose, a hands-on tech enthusiast, software builder, and full-stack developer with a passion for lifelong learning. With over 20 years of experience in the software industry, I’ve recently pivoted my career to focus on advanced tech skills, personal growth, and health.

Professional Journey

During my 12-year tenure at Intuit, I led the Engineering organization for QuickBooks Platform, QuickBooks Mobile, and Gen AI. As the Head of Engineering, I was responsible for driving strategy and overseeing the development of QuickBooks mobile applications and the platform infrastructure hosting QuickBooks Online. I worked closely with Product, Design, and Marketing leaders to deliver best-in-class solutions across the QuickBooks ecosystem.

In 2023, I made a career pivot and embarked on a Reimagine Journey to shift my focus from engineering leadership to hands-on technology. This extended leave gave me the opportunity to reflect on my passions and shape the next chapter of my career.

In August 2024, after an incredible 14-year journey at Intuit, I made the bittersweet decision to leave Intuit to lead a life dedicated to learning, coding, and building tech solutions to real-life problems without the constraints of a full-time job.
